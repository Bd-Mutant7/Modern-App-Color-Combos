## 🎯 Overview

Color combinations optimized for music streaming and audio applications, focusing on energy, mood, and immersive experiences.

### Why These Colors Work
| Palette | Emotion | Best For |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Primary** | Energy, Creativity | Music players, playlists |
| **Dark Mode** | Immersion, Battery | Listening at night |
| **Luxury** | Premium, Exclusive | Hi-fi audio, concerts |
