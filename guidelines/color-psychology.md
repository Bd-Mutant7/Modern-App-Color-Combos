## Color Meanings in Healthcare

### Teal (#4ECDC4)
**Emotion:** Trust, Calm, Professionalism
**Use Cases:**
- Primary CTAs
- Navigation bars
- Headers
- Trust indicators

### Coral (#FF6B6B)
**Emotion:** Urgency, Alert, Attention
**Use Cases:**
- Error messages
- Urgent notifications
- Abnormal test results
- Emergency buttons

### Mint (#A8E6CF)
**Emotion:** Growth, Health, Success
**Use Cases:**
- Normal test results
- Progress indicators
- Achievement badges
- Positive outcomes

### Slate Gray (#2C3E50)
**Emotion:** Stability, Professionalism
**Use Cases:**
- Body text
- Icons
- Secondary information
- Form labels
